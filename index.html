About FitLife Gambia

Your partner in health and fitness since 2018

Our Story

FitLife Gambia was founded with a single mission: to make quality fitness accessible to everyone in The Gambia. What started as a small gym in Serrekunda has grown into one of the country's most trusted fitness center.

We beleive that fitness is not just about building muscles or losing weight. It's about confidence, improving health, and creating a community of people who support each other on their wellness journey.

Our Facilities

Our modern facility offers everything you need for a complete workout:

  • Over 50 pieces of cardio equipment including treadmills, bikes and elipticals
  • Full free weight area with dumbells up to 50kg
  • Dedicated group fitness studio for classes
  • Clean and specious locker rooms with showers
  • Air-conditioned throughout for your comfort
